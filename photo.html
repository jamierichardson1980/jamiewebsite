Photography

I recently went backpacking and volunteering for 4 years, in various locations around our planet.
Not particularly content with the life i was experiencing in the UK, I decided to look for happiness further afield, and attempt a nomad lifestyle.
I tried to capture as much of that journey as I could via photography and video.
Hopefully from this selection of images, you can get a sense of some of the more unbelievable things I experienced on those travels.
