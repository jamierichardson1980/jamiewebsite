A Normal Life

A massive, personal, interest in video gaming since I was 4 years old and competent enough to whack a space bar or waggle a joystick.
I've owned or used just about every major franchise, system or computer since the 80's.
Played all the arcades during the 90's..
..and have developed unhealthy obsessions with online gaming since the noughties.
I have been involved in, and organised events for, several, large, gaming communities throughout the years.
These include 15-20 person LAN gatherings, tournaments through steam and meetups for MMO's I've played.
I have also created youtube and twitch content. Some of which you can find links to further down this page.

Recently, I started a crash course in programming and as part of a project, created a fully functional, text based, adventure game.
The game uses roguelike elements and randomisations to allow the player to choose different routes through to the end.
You can play this game below.
Some elements may not display properly due to variations in people's setups, but you can just click the big green run button to start the game!
